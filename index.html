Scotland

Taste it, Hike it, Enjoy it!

Scotland is beautiful in every season and it holds thousands of opportunities for visitors. You will find historical sites, myths, culinary experiences and modern cities at the same time in the northern part of the British Isles.

I was lucky because I could visit this great country with my friends some years ago. This webpage contains information which most of them based on own experience.These information would help for tourists who will visit Scotland.
